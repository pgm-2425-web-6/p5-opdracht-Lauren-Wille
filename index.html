glow tetra

Glow tetra fish (Hemigrammus erythrozonus or GloFish Tetra) are small, vibrant freshwater fish originally from South America, often genetically enhanced to display neon colors like red, green, or blue. They are peaceful, social fish that thrive in groups and prefer tanks with dim lighting to highlight their glow.

gold fish

Goldfish (Carassius auratus), one of the most popular aquarium pets, are hardy freshwater fish with bright orange, gold, or white colors, known for their friendly behavior and ability to grow larger than expected in spacious tanks.

cichlid

Cichlids, a diverse family of freshwater fish, include popular species like angelfish and Oscars, known for their striking patterns, intelligent behavior, and territorial nature, requiring spacious tanks and careful species selection to ensure harmony.
